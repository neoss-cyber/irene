Help

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about your stay

schedule What are the check-in and check-out times?

Check-in is from 3:00 PM and check-out is until 11:00 AM. Early check-in or late check-out may be available upon request, subject to availability.

directions_car Is parking available?

We offer nearby parking options for our guests. Please contact us in advance to arrange parking, and we'll provide you with the best options available.

cancel What is your cancellation policy?

Free cancellation is available up to 48 hours before your scheduled arrival. Cancellations made within 48 hours may be subject to a charge.

pets Are pets allowed?

Pets are welcome in select apartments. Please contact us before booking to confirm availability and any additional fees that may apply.

restaurant Is breakfast included?

Breakfast can be arranged upon request. We also have a fully equipped kitchen in each apartment where you can prepare your own meals.

airport_shuttle Do you offer airport transfers?

Yes, we can arrange airport transfers for our guests. Please provide your flight details when booking, and we'll take care of the rest.
