Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about your stay

schedule What are the check-in and check-out times?

Check-in is from 3:00 PM and check-out is until 11:00 AM. Early check-in or late check-out may be available upon request, subject to availability.

family_restroom Is the apartment family-friendly?

Yes, families are welcome. Please let us know if you require a baby cot.

pets Are pets allowed?

Pets may be allowed upon request. Please contact us in advance to confirm availability and any applicable fees or conditions.

wifi Do you offer WiFi?

Yes, we provide high-speed Starlink WiFi throughout the property, ensuring fast and reliable internet access during your stay.

airport_shuttle Do you offer airport transfers?

Yes, we can arrange airport transfers for our guests. Please provide your flight details when booking, and we'll take care of the rest.

accessible Is the property suitable for guests with mobility limitations?

Due to stairs and the building layout, the property may not be suitable for guests with limited mobility. Please contact us before booking if you have specific accessibility needs.
